## Ingredients: 

- 1 cup Modern Table Elbows
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 medium white onion (about 1 cup, chopped)
- 2 stalks celery (about 1 cup, chopped)
- 2 medium carrots (about 1 cup, chopped)
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 medium bay leaves
- 28 oz can diced tomato
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 2 medium zucchini (about 1.5 cups, chopped)
- 1 cup chopped green beans
- 1 can cannellini beans, rinsed and drained
- 2 cups fresh baby spinach

### Steps

1. Start by turning the instant pot on sauté mode for 6 minutes. Allow the instant pot to warm up 1-2 minutes
2. Add olive oil and the chopped mirepoix (onion & garlic). Stir well and allow to sauté until the Instant Pot beeps from the 7 minute timer.
3. Add the rest of the ingredients except the pasta and the spinach and put the lid on the instant pot.
4. Cook on high pressure for 1 minute. Manually release immediately.
5. Turn the instant pot back on sauté mode for 5 minutes. Add the pasta and cook for 4 minutes
6. After 4 minutes, add the spinach and stir well. Serve hot!
